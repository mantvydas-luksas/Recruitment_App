Recruitment Simplified

Let us select candidates on behalf of you with the following list of features below...

Search For Talent

WorkNow allows you to post a job advert for any city in Ireland!
Simply, login with your details, fill out the details of the job description, consisting of all the requirements you are looking for.

Automatic CV Evaluation

Thanks to our AI technology, if a candidate satisfies the requirements that you are looking for, the details of the candidate are passed on to you automatically, if you wish to evaluate it further.

Have a list of successful candidates at your disposal, what's next? Have you got to a schedule a meeting now ?

Not at all! Let us send a reminder to each successful candidate of the scheduled interview meeting.
